What Is Spinal Flow Technique?

Spinal Flow Technique is a gentle, non-invasive healing modality that works with your body's innate intelligence.

Using light contacts on specific access points along the spine, the practitioner helps the nervous system release stored stress, tension, and emotional blockages so your body can return to a greater state of balance and healing.

Sessions are performed fully clothed and are relaxing, comfortable, and suitable for people of all ages.
